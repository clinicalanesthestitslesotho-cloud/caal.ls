About CAAL

The Clinical Anaesthetists Association of Lesotho (CAAL) represents professionals dedicated to safe and effective anaesthetic care across the country.

Clinical anaesthetists are trained healthcare professionals responsible for administering anaesthesia, monitoring patients, and ensuring safety before, during, and after medical procedures.

Our Vision

To uphold strong ethical standards and promote excellence in anaesthesia practice and leadership.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, safe, and holistic anaesthetic care while supporting the welfare and development of anaesthetists nationwide.
